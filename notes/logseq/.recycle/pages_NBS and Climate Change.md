tags:: NbS

- NbS for climate change mitigation
- NbS for climate adaptation
	- 1) reducing socioeconomic exposure (reduce flood risk,..)
	- 2) sensitivity (people adapt better to environmental shicks)
	- 3) capacity (genetic and specie diversity)
- Difficulties with NbS:
	- Measuring of effectiveness, hard to create a metric or indicator for social-ecological effectiveness of NbS
	- Cost-effectiveness
		- NbS appear to not have a major effects for most extreme events
		- trade-offs among different interventions and ecosystem services or between stakeholder groups, may experience costs and benefit differently
		- response of ecosystem much harder to predict
		- mix of interventions may help stakeholders
